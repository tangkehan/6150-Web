I'm friendly!

Friendly is my middle name. When my humans come home, I can't help but shower them with affection, purring and nuzzling as if to say, "I've missed you!" I love being the center of attention and getting ear scritches.

I'm Sesame!

I'm Sesame (芝麻), the 4-month-old feline explorer. My humans are my world, and I've got them wrapped around my fluffy paw. Life through my eyes is a playful adventure, a world filled with love and sunshine.
